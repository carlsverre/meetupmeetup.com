Code of Conduct

Meetup Meetup is a space for meetup organizers to connect, share experiences, and learn from each other. Our events are social and collaborative, and we aim to keep them welcoming, thoughtful, and productive for everyone.

If a host determines that this code of conduct has been breached, we may take appropriate action. This can include a private conversation, a warning, or asking someone to leave or not attend future events. If you experience or witness an issue, please talk to a host or email us at meetupmeetup@fastmail.com. We will respect your privacy.

TL;DR

  • Be kind, collaborative, and friendly.
  • Respect people, boundaries, and the space.
  • Follow session guidelines and respect host facilitation.
  • Ask before self-promoting.

Our principles

Shared responsibility for the experience

Everyone plays a role in making Meetup Meetup a positive experience. Please contribute constructively, be mindful of others, and help create a welcoming and supportive environment.

Respect each other

Meetup organizers come from different backgrounds, communities, and experiences. Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, discrimination, or aggressive behavior is not acceptable. This includes unwelcome comments about someone's identity, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption of sessions, and unwanted physical contact.

Respect boundaries

  • If someone does not want to engage, respect that.
  • If conversation or outreach is not reciprocated, back off.
  • This applies to professional, platonic, and romantic interactions.

Respect the space

  • Clean up after yourself.
  • No theft or damage to property.
  • Follow any venue-specific rules.

Respect confidentiality

Conversations at Meetup Meetup may include sensitive topics about communities or personal experiences. Please don't share what others have said outside the event without their permission.

Photos and recording

We may take photos or videos at events. If you'd prefer not to be photographed or recorded, let a host know and we'll respect that. Please also respect others who have opted out.

Follow session guidelines

  • Be mindful of talks, discussions, and collaborative activities.
  • Avoid interrupting speakers or dominating conversations.
  • Keep discussions on topic when the session calls for it.
  • Self-promotion is welcome only when explicitly invited or approved by a host.

Talk to the hosts

If something feels off, uncomfortable, or unclear, let a host know or email meetupmeetup@fastmail.com. We care about keeping Meetup Meetup a supportive space and will handle concerns discreetly.

Accessibility

Some of our event spaces may not be fully accessible. If you have specific accessibility needs, please reach out ahead of time so we can do our best to accommodate you.
